NEW CHAPTERS IN NISIG e.V

Over the years, our scholars and professionals keep on increasing and occupying all cities in Germany to achieve their academic/career pursuits. In a bid to cater for their integration and their timely needs , the current administration created a committee to look into the creation of new chapters and expand the scope of NiSIG e.V to these areas.

The committee have worked to the best of their ability and five new chapters have been added to the currently existing 13 chapters of NiSIG e.V. This has increased the chapters within the association to 18.

Below are the new chapters of the Association and the respective coordinators. The National Executives will offer all necessary support to these new chapters and ensure that all scholars in these regions are well catered for in all ramifications.

NiSIG Damstadt Chapter
Chapter Coordinator : Mr Taiwo Samuel Oluwafemi

NiSIG Kassel Chapter
ChapterCoordinator: Mrs Onyinyechi Ajaegbu

NiSIG Oldenburg Chapter
Coordinator: Miss Adenike Adenaya

NiSIG Frankfurt Oder
Coordinator: Mr Abayomi Medemaku

NiSIG Rhein/Main Chapter
Coordinator: Miss Faith Obioma

Please visit for more information. We humbly urge all our members in these regions to work in unity and strive to achieve the objectives of the association bearing in mind that the Association will always come through for them in time of need. The National secretariat humbly plead with the leaders of these chapters to promote love and peaceful coexistence in their administrative duties;upholding the constitution of the Association and prioritizing the welfarism of the members above all.
The administration alongside the inauguration committee will not relent in the creation of new chapters and expanding the scope of NiSIG e.V for the benefit of our members far and near. In case we have our members/prospective members in any of the above mentioned cities,kindly send a message to the National Executives (info@nisig.de)and you will be added to the respective chapter’s platforms.
Thank you for your interest and dedication towards the growth and development of NiSIG e.V.
