Nigerian Scholars invite you...

Since the inception of Nigerian Scholars in Germany (NiSIG e.V), we have a tradition of having an anuual reunion. An arena for learning, development and networking.

Just like in 2020, the 2021 Summit would be held virtually, however, this promises to be educative, informative and entertaining.

Our speaking line up for this year is first class.

Every year, we try to improve on the quality of speakers that we bring to the summit. This year's summit is not an exception.
